Your credit score significantly impacts the loans you qualify for:

  • 670-739 - Good: Competitive rates
  • 580-669 - Fair: Higher interest rates
  • Below 580 - Poor: Limited options

Income/Debt

  • Some personal loans allow up to 50%

Collateral

Loan Terminology Explained

APR

Annual Percentage Rate - includes interest + fees

DTI

Debt-to-Income ratio - monthly debts ÷ gross income

LTV

Loan-to-Value - loan amount ÷ asset value

Amortization

Gradual repayment through scheduled payments
